Mission

To provide cost-effective products to Africans by integrating resources from a global perspective.

Corporate Vision

To become a respected industrial and trade enterprise in Africa with limited but diverse businesses; to become the leader in our focused field. To maximize the value of our customers and employees by adhering to the spirit of being brave to win and daring to win.

Corporate Values

Customers First

Customer-centered and think from their point of view. Serve customers, invest in customers, and continue to create value for customers. Honesty is the foundation of our business; we keep our promises.

Professionalism and Innovation

We are determined to provide cost-effective products in Africa and resolve to discover new commercial value for customers and suppliers. We will develop ourselves with an uncomplacent attitude, dare to break old infractions, innovate new ones, surpass ourselves, maintain unlimited energy and constant curiosity. Through the innovation of production methods, product innovation, channel innovation, service innovation, and management innovation, our company can achieve victory.

Teamwork

Establish the awareness of effective execution; execute first, discuss them, realize the strategies during execution. Don't bring personal biases to the workplace; focus on the issue rather than the person; pay attention to the overall situation and always think about what is right. Trust makes things simpler; create and share together; ordinary people can achieve extraordinary things.

Embrace Change

Change is the only constant rule in the world. Let's change as needed, keep on challenging ourselves; embrace changes in positions, job scope, and be competent for the change. Facing changes, we should actively accept and adjust ourselves to break through and improve ourselves. Create changes and bring breakthrough results.

Keep Learning

Live and learn; the challenges of work and life are everywhere, and the process of learning is to constantly improve. Only by continuous learning and updating our knowledge at all times can we be bold in the face of new situations and changes. We know that we may refuse to learn, but our opponents will not. Acknowledge failures and be courageous; be willing to criticize ourselves and keep on making self-examination and make progress by helping others and achieving ourselves.

Happy Work

Be critical with ourselves and be tolerant to others; be grateful with people and things around. Be optimistic and full of love for the company, work, and colleagues; regardless of gains and losses, devote yourself to the work and always influence colleagues and the team with optimism. Accept the challenges of work and live with joy and passion. Be courageous to overcome difficulties.
